Blood donation eligiblity

  • Age of donor must be above 18 and below 65 years.
  • Body weight not less than 45 kgs.
  • Haemoglobin count that is not less than 12.5 g/dl.
  • Body Temperature-Normal (oral temperature not exceeding 37.50C).
  • Pulse of donor shall be normal.
  • The systolic and diastolic blood pressures are within normal limits.

About us

A Blood Donation System is a web-based application designed to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients, facilitating the efficient and timely availability of blood. The system aims to address challenges such as blood shortages, inefficient donor-recipient matching, and delays in urgent situations.
